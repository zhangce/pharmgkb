We have screened a subset of 300 presumable X-linked families by X chromosome-specific array-CGH and identi- fied five families with overlapping microduplications at Xpl1.22. Subsequent screening of additional patients by qPCR identified one more positive family. The common duplicated region contains two candidate genes, the hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase HSD17B10 (also known as HADH2, MHBD, or ABAD) and the E3 ubiquitin ligase HUWE1 (also known as UREB1), both of which showed overexpression in the affected individuals.
